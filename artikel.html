India

The contradiction of the economic condition of the people between rich and poor, Indian economists to judge their own country as a country of contradictions. The Indian government's efforts to bring the country's economy to the better have not been followed by an increase or even improvement in the living standards of most of its people living in poverty. Simultaneously with economic reforms and implementation of government policies of this country in the economic sector, Globalization is developing and influential in it.

The progress of several vital sectors due to globalization is able to provide its own 'opportunity' for some elements of Indian society to improve their 'standard of living'. The Government of India formed a classification among the lower society in India into 3 parts, namely: Scheduled Tribes, Scheduled Castes, and Other Backward Castes.

Most poverty in India comes from scheduled castes and scheduled tribes who are generally domiciled in rural areas.
